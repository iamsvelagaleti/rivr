    OutputTurn, JsonSerializable

    public class SubdialogueCall
extends VoiceXmlOutputTurn
    A SubdialogueCall is a VoiceXmlOutputTurn that invokes another external dialogue.

    Parameters may be passed to the subdialogue and return values may be retrieved if the invoked subdialogue ends with a <return> instruction.

    See Also:
    SubdialogueCall.Parameter, https://www.w3.org/TR/voicexml20/#dml2.3.4
