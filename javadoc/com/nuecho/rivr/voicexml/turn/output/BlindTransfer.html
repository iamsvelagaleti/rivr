    public class BlindTransfer
extends Transfer
    A BlindTransfer is a Transfer where the outcome is not monitored.

    Once the transfer begins and the interpreter disconnects from the session, the platform throws connection.disconnect.transfer.

    See Also:
    https://www.w3.org/TR/voicexml20/#dml2.3.7.1
