    public abstract class SupervisedTransfer
extends Transfer
    A SupervisedTransfer is a Transfer where the outcome is monitored and where a transfer failure can be handled gracefully.

    Recognition can optionally be activated to cancel a supervised transfer.

    See Also:
    BridgeTransfer, ConsultationTransfer
